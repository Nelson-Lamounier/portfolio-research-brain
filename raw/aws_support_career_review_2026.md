## Automation Project Design — Case Distribution System (IN PROGRESS)

**Status:** Architectural design complete — pending formal approval and security review
**Goal:** Automate the end-to-end lifecycle of case distribution to replace current manual workflow

### Problem Statement

The current manual case distribution process is inefficient and unsustainable:
- **Time cost:** 10–20 hours per week lost in the Dublin region alone
- **Pressure:** Weekend periods require disproportionate manual effort (example: 3 agents
  handling 21 cases requiring manual distribution and tracking)
- **Fragmentation:** Staff must manually copy and paste data across 4 platforms
  (case management system, spreadsheet, collaborative document tool, Slack) twice daily

### Technical Design

Scripted automation handling the end-to-end case distribution lifecycle:
- **Resource management module:** Pulls currently available team members automatically
- **Data retrieval module:** Fetches open cases filtered by monitoring tags from case console
- **Distribution logic:** Applies fair case allocation algorithm across available team
- **Communication module:** Dispatches Slack notifications to relevant parties automatically
- **Tool:** "Dante Scripts" automation framework

### Business Impact

- Eliminates 10–20 hours/week manual overhead in EMEA region
- Reduces stress during high-volume periods (weekends/holidays)
- Improves team work-life balance
- Global rollout potential: if validated in Dublin, saves hundreds of hours/week globally
- Improves customer experience through faster, consistent case assignment

### Project Status

- Formal business requirements documentation: complete
- ROI analysis: complete
- Technical approach documentation: complete
- Performance metrics impact analysis: complete
- Dependencies: requires senior management sponsorship + security review
- Nelson committed to hands-on implementation phase upon approval

*Resume-ready framing:* "Identified a 10–20 hours/week manual overhead in regional case
management and designed a scripted automation system to replace the workflow end-to-end —
resource querying, data retrieval, distribution logic, and Slack notification dispatch.
Authored full business case and ROI analysis. Global rollout candidate pending
security approval."
